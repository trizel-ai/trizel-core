## DECLARATION

Pursuant to the completion of Layer-0 (Governance), Layer-1 (Research Documentation), and Layer-2 (Presentation), the TRIZEL project is hereby declared to be in a **GLOBAL FREEZE** state.

This Freeze is binding across all TRIZEL repositories and organizations.

---

## SCOPE OF FREEZE

The FREEZE applies comprehensively to:

1. **Layer-0 (Governance)** - Governance framework is closed and stable
2. **Layer-1 (Research Documentation)** - Research documentation is frozen
3. **Layer-2 (Presentation)** - Presentation layer is frozen

---

## PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

During the FREEZE, the following activities are strictly prohibited without explicit Scientific Authorization:

1. **Scientific Production** - No new scientific research, analysis, or experimentation
2. **Scientific Execution** - No execution of scientific workflows or computational studies
3. **Scientific Analysis** - No interpretation, analysis, or synthesis of scientific data
4. **Scientific Publication** - No submission, release, or publication of scientific findings
5. **Dataset Creation** - No creation, modification, or release of scientific datasets
6. **DOI Minting** - No minting of Digital Object Identifiers for scientific artifacts
7. **Zenodo Archiving** - No archiving of scientific records to Zenodo
8. **Citation Generation** - No generation of citations for scientific work
