## FREEZE STATEMENT

Following the completion of Layer-2 (Presentation) initialization, this document declares an explicit **FREEZE** on all Layer-2 repositories and scientific activities within the TRIZEL project.

No scientific authorization has been issued.

---

## FREEZE SCOPE

### Layer-1 (Research Documentation)
- Research documentation may exist internally
- Research documentation is **NOT AUTHORIZED** for publication
- No public release of research findings is permitted

### Layer-2 (Presentation)
- Presentation layer is permanently non-scientific
- Limited to documentation and presentation of governance
- No scientific content is authorized in Layer-2

### All Layers
- All Layer-2 repositories are frozen
- No scientific content is authorized
- No research publication is permitted
- No DOI / Zenodo / citation is allowed

---

## SCIENTIFIC AUTHORIZATION STATUS

**Status:** NOT GRANTED

No scientific authorization has been issued for the TRIZEL project.

Any scientific activity requires a future explicit authorization document issued through the governance framework.

---

## PROHIBITED ACTIONS DURING FREEZE

The following actions are prohibited during the FREEZE period:

1. **Scientific Publication** - No publication of scientific findings or research
2. **DOI Minting** - No minting of Digital Object Identifiers
3. **Zenodo Archiving** - No archiving of scientific records
4. **Citation Generation** - No generation of scientific citations
5. **Research Release** - No public release of research documentation from Layer-1
6. **Scientific Analysis** - No execution or publication of scientific analysis
7. **Dataset Publication** - No publication of scientific datasets

---

## AUTHORIZATION REQUIREMENT

Any future scientific publication requires:

1. Explicit Scientific Authorization Declaration
2. Formal governance approval process
3. Compliance with all applicable policies
4. Evidence of completed authorization workflow

Until such authorization is granted, all scientific activities remain prohibited.

---

## COMPLIANCE

This FREEZE is effective immediately and remains in force until explicitly lifted by governance authority.

All Pull Requests must comply with FREEZE restrictions.
